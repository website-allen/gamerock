NOVA Legacy tutorials

In this tutorial we are going to share with you tips and tricks that will make you progress faster in the game.


  1. How to get maxed out fast?


    2. One can get maxedout in less than 4months by coinfarming, playing multiplayer and events. coinfarming will generate more coins, events will return valuable cards and multiplayer will return some few rewards.


  2. What is coinfarming and how to do it ?


    3. Coinfarming is the process of getting coins faster in the game. It is done by using rocket launchers (advisably two) and going to the first mission of new ceres jungle and kill the enemies from the heliport. By doing this, you will obtain RPG kill (+50 coins ) for each successful shot.The image below explains better.

    For coin farming to be successful, its advisable to use boom and ignition as boom has the largest magazine size followed by ignition.Also, its advisable that boom should be upgraded to at least 220 and ignition 170.All these are from experience.


  3. What gear combination to use?


    4. If you want to get maxedout ontime your are advised to use gears from epic cards. The easiest combination is saggititarius and juggernaut.Also, you must know that the snipers are the most powerful weapons. If you want to be faster while you move in the game you are advised to use cheetah or nightcore.However cheetah is easier to max out just like juggernaut. However, the most powerful and reliable combination is Arachnid and Titan or Nightcore. here is a video on gear combination


  4. Events are so tough!


    5. Events are meant to be tough but as a noob, the pros wont let you see the light to make things better, you are advised to start events last six hours or last four hours or even 1day 1hour There was a time i started rush elite last 30 mins and i got ten packs. Although you must know your event server is random.


  5. What avatar to use ?


    6. Its ok to use any avatar but some avatar have specs e.g prometheus's spec is invisibility usually in alien city. Ludo's spec is that sometimes his head is hard to get.One advantage of collecting cards to unlock avatars is the coins generated from the excess.


  6. How to camp


    7. Campimg requires skill and experience but as a beginner i advise you to find a favourite spot usually close to a spawn point and practice with it.you can have many favourite spots.you can as well deal with campers by attacking from their blind spots or using katana even rpg. You must know snipers are campers choice.

  7. How to gain ratings


    8. Ratings can be gained when you take first position in a match with the most kills and the least deaths especially against players with good amount of ratings.


  8. How to double packs.


    9. Lets say you have 20packs and you wish to have them doubled, to do so, open one pack and turn off your mobile data/wifi depending on which you are using. After that, open all the packs and wait at least 1min (advisably 2mins) then tap open.Magically, you will be able to open another 19packs.Here's a video on
    how to double packs


  9. How to change game server.


    10. Firstly, there a two servers in this game, European and asian server. European, African and some other countries are defaulted to European server while Asian server are of the Asian countries. To change your server, Delete the game files and connect to a country via vpn that has the server you want. eg America to European server and India to asian server.After that, open the game and go online. This is a video on how to change the game's server.
